Task 1. The names of two children and the number of candies they have are entered from the keyboard. Display the name of the child who has the most candies, or display that the number is the same.
Task 2. The price of the product and the amount of money are entered from the keyboard. If there is not enough money, we refuse the purchase, otherwise, if there is still money left, we offer to buy a lottery ticket for 4 UAH.
Task 3. A number from 1 to 5 is randomly generated. Try to guess the number in 2 attempts.
Task 4. The person's age is entered from the keyboard. The screen displays who they are (kindergarten child, schoolchild, student, employee, pensioner).
Task 5. The name of the driver's category is entered from the keyboard (A-motorcycle, B-car, C-truck). Display the name of the vehicle he can drive on the screen.
Task 6. Enter the number of the day of the week from the keyboard. Display the name of the day on the screen.
Task 7. The month number is entered from the keyboard. Display which season it belongs to.
